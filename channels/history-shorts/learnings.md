# history-shorts — channel learnings

The channel's memory: the script writer, the direction judge and the SEO
strategist read the TAIL of this file. The interactive `make` session appends
your script critiques here automatically; you can also add lines by hand. Run
`python3 kalinga.py --channel history-shorts condense-learnings` when it grows.

- [seed · craft] Open INSIDE the moment at its most charged instant, never with
  a date or "let's travel back". Put the viewer on the wall of Constantinople,
  not in a lecture.
- [seed · craft] One vivid, specific detail beats three facts — the smell of the
  harbour, the exact 20 minutes, the single misread signal. Cut the list.
- [seed · accuracy] Real names, real dates, no invented quotes. If the record is
  uncertain or contested, say so briefly rather than smoothing it into a myth.
