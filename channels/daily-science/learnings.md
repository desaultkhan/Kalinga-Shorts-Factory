# daily-science — channel learnings

This file is the channel's memory. The script writer, the direction judge and
the SEO strategist all read the TAIL of it, so lessons you record here shape
future videos. The interactive `make` session appends your script critiques
here automatically; you can also add lines by hand. Keep the highest-value
rules near the end (run `python3 kalinga.py condense-learnings` when it grows).

## Openings
- [seed · craft] Lead with the concrete phenomenon, never a framing line —
  open on the thing happening ("A drop of water hits the pan and skitters
  instead of boiling off…"), not "Have you ever wondered…".

## Explanation craft
- [seed · craft] One analogy per video, and glue it to something the viewer
  can physically picture. Two competing analogies muddy the mental model.

## Accuracy
- [seed · accuracy] Never invent a number. If the research facts don't give a
  figure, describe the effect qualitatively rather than fabricating precision.
